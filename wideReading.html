Welcome to the website for our wide reading assignment! We have chosen the book True History of the Kelly Gang, written by Peter Carey. This book shows the surprising true history of the murderer who we know as Ned Kelly. To his people, Kelly and his gang were heroes for opposing the english. To the english, Kelly and his gang were cold-blooded, cruel serial killers. Now, for the first time, we can take a glimpse at his life, scribbled down hastily by Ned Kelly himself as he runs from the police.
