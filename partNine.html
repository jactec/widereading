Part 9


In this chapter, there is a manhunt for the Kelly Gang as they are regarded as criminals and have been spotted in the Bullock Creek area together. The hunt is initiated and lead by policeman Alex Fitzpatrick. The policeman that used to be their friend had betrayed them in return for money. They find and attempt to apprehend the Kelly Gang but are outgunned after a decisive victory, leaving Alex Fitzpatrick dead upon the ground. Later it is revealed that the gang were ordered to be apprehended fatally (dead) instead of normally (alive).

Following this, the Kelly Gang is continuously pursued across the country and is forced to exchange fire in many circumstances including one very significant exchange of fire at Stringybark Creek where several policemen were injured in combat or killed. These series of events is known as the Stringybark Creek Murders.
